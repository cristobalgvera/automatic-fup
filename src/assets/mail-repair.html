Dear supplier:

It is important for LATAM that your company keep us updated on the status of all the open repair orders.

We will begin to periodically every 15 days send a form (attached) with a LATAM-defined format meant to keep track of our open orders. We ask that you follow the instructions included in the attached file and to keep us informed of the status of our components through this tool.

Please consider these status to answer and include the required information in each of them:

  • Not received: In case you have not been informed of this order yet, or you have not received our unit to be inspected/quoted.
  • Core Return: Include serial number exchange in the comments section.
  • To be quoted: In case you have not sent the quote yet. Include the estimated delivery date (format: dd/mm/yyyy).
  • Awaiting quote approval: In case LATAM has not approved the quote yet. Include quote number in the comments section.
  • Awaiting CIA payment: In case there is a pending CIA payment. Include the invoice number in the comments section.
  • Under repair process: In case the material is in your dependencies. Include the estimated delivery date (format: dd/mm/yyyy).
  • Shipped: In case you have already sent this material to LATAM. Include the AWB.
  • Scrapped: In case the material has been requested to be scrapped on site. Include the scrap certification number in the comments section.

We request for your reply to take no longer than 3 working days after receiving the form.

We trust in your support to help us in our supply chain process and reduce the impact in our passengers.

Best regards,

Supplier Management
